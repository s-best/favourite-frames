FAVOURITE FRAMES

What is this?

Favourite Frames is a bit of a blog. Semi-regularly I'll be posting selections of my favourite stills from films I've seen and provide a short write up as to why. I'd alse be super interested to hear your opinions.

Why is this?

Great question friend. I love movies, we all do. I also have a passion for photography, so why not break it down and take a look at some great stills. I looked around and couldn't see too many other places doing this.

When is this?

It's sometimes, that's for sure. This is pretty new.
